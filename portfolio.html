Pickle's Prehistoric Bites

This website was one of the last websites I made as a CART Web Apps student. As you can tell, there's a lot of functionality compared to other, more earlier projects. I first had to choose what kind of “full-service” website we wanted to make and who we were working with. I then had to conduct a competitive audit to see what competitors did well, and what they could improve on. The overall purpose of this project was to give InGen, the company from Jurassic Park, a website for a multitude of attractions in their park. During development, there were a lot of issues. Namely, the navigation bar with the logo and the “hamburger” icon. They never seemed to fit just right and always had something wrong with their sizing or positioning. Another problem was some of the images not sizing up correctly and would appear pixelated. Although there were a lot of parts that I loved about the project, mainly the humor I felt when I was able to make an entire restaurant's website based on a running joke I made up. Another part would be the JavaScript needed to make an image carousel with some of the foods in the restaurant. Overall rating, 9/10.
