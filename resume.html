Volunteering:
Virtually every Sunday since December of 2022, I have volunteered at Valley Animal Center (VAC). At the adoption center, I usually do odd-jobs, such as sweeping, changing linens, and swapping out water and food. After 3 months, I decided to also volunteer at their feral yard, which is outside. I would volunteer at the adoption center for an hour, then go to the feral yard for 2 hours, since the outside wasn't maintained as much as the inside. As of 2024, VAC is renovating the feral yard and allowing some of the outside cats to be adopted due to the help I have given to keep the feral yard sustained.
