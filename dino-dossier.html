Dino Dossier: Spinosaurus

This was one of the first websites I made for a project at CART. It really shows graphically and informationally. I had to scour through a lot of websites claiming to have info on the Spinosaurus, only for it to be about some subspecies of it or just overall knowledge every other site used. A hard part of the website was introducing a “carousel” of images. It was difficult since the website was made in the first semester of CART and we learned JavaScript in the second semester. After going through countless websites, I was ready to compile all of the information into HTML. This project was good in the sense of what it felt like to make a website from scratch, and put gathered information there. I also had a lot of fun making the website since we didn't have to worry about looks or functionality. Overall rating, 8.5/10.
